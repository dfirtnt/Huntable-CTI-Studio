Metrics

  • Precision — matched ÷ actual extracted — low false positives
  • Recall — matched ÷ expected — low missed items
  • Matched — in both expected and extracted
  • Missed — in expected but not extracted (false negatives)
  • Extra — extracted but not in expected (false positives)

Score colors

  • 90%+ — Excellent
  • 70–89% — Good
  • <70% — Needs work
  • n/a — Not annotated or pending

Notes

  • Click any completed row to see missed and extra item lists.
  • Only the most recent completed run per article is shown.
  • Matching normalizes to lowercase, defangs [.]→., collapses whitespace.
  • count only — article has no expected_items annotation yet.
